About Mirissa Nanny & Baby Care

Caring for traveling families in paradise

Our Story

Helping Families Enjoy Sri Lanka, Worry-Free

Mirissa Nanny & Baby Care was founded with a simple mission: to help international families make the most of their time in beautiful Mirissa without worrying about childcare or household chores.

We understand the unique challenges parents face when traveling — unfamiliar surroundings, disrupted routines, and the need for trustworthy help. Our team brings years of hands-on experience caring for children of all ages, from infants to pre-teens.

Based in Mirissa, we serve families staying at hotels, villas, and guesthouses across the Southern coast. Whether you're here for a week or a month, we're here to support you every step of the way.

Our comprehensive approach goes beyond babysitting — we offer complete household support so you can truly relax and explore everything Sri Lanka has to offer.
