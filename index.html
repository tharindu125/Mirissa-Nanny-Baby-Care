About Us

Professional Care You Can Trust

At Mirissa Nanny & Baby Care, we understand how important it is for parents to have reliable support while traveling. Our dedicated team brings years of experience in baby care and housekeeping to ensure both children and homes are well taken care of.

Based in beautiful Mirissa, we serve international families staying at hotels and villas along the Southern coast. Our goal is simple: give you the freedom to explore Sri Lanka while we keep your family comfortable and your home in order.
